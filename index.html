About Us

Many years, Bethany has been baking the most delicious pies at her home.

In 2013, we opened our first store in Brusel (Belgium), in a cosy little street near the Grand Market.

People from all over the world, fell in love with the soft cakes, heavenly cheese cakes and spicy apple pies.

The store became a success in no time.

After recieving many requests, it became clear that our next step was making it possible to order pies, from the comfort of your own home, and from anywhere in the world.

You are now looking at the result of this, our very own webstore, making it possible to order Bethany's delicious pies whenever you feel like.
